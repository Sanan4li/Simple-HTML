Why Students Choose Us ?

Arshad Institute of technology is the only institute in Gujrat giving quality education to all students. Our institute is affliated with PBTE (Punjab board of Technical Education ) Lahore. Our students have got top positions in District Gujrat and in the Pubject Board of Technical Education as well. We have high qualified staff in institute to teach all the major and minor subjects on the courses. We have well organized computer labs , workshops for practicals and a very good building which in the center of Gujrat. Read More.
